Voice Exercise

A gentle, guided exercise to help strengthen your voice. We'll measure your progress as you go.

What to expect
🌬️
5 short rounds of vocal exercise
⏱️
10 seconds per round — just say "Ahhh"
😌
Rest between each round — take your time
📊
Results summary at the end

How to do it

Follow these steps for each round

1
Sit comfortably and take a slow, deep breath in through your nose.
2
Say a steady "Ahhh" — as long, even, and clear as you can manage.
3
Watch the volume bar and keep your voice in the green zone.
4
When the round ends, breathe gently and rest before the next one.
